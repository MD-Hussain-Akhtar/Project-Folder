About Us

Welcome to the Arha Help Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to making a difference in our community. Our mission is to provide essential support and resources to those in need, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to thrive. Founded by Hussain Akhter, our foundation is committed to fostering positive change and creating a more equitable society. Join us in our journey to bring hope and help to those who need it most.

Arha Help Foundation

The Arha Help Foundation aims to improve life in villages by addressing common issues like water supply and maintaining the village mosque. The foundation provides water coolers, assists with repairs and equipment for the mosque, and helps anyone in need within the community.

By bringing the village together, the foundation ensures support in emergencies or when someone requires financial or material aid, making the village a better place for all.
