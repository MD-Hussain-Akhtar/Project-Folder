About Us

This website has been created by Hussain Akhter with the goal of supporting the mission of the Arha Help Foundation. As the founder, Hussain Akhter envisioned a platform where the foundation's efforts to uplift and empower the underprivileged could be highlighted. The website serves as a bridge between the foundation and the community, providing essential information about the organization’s initiatives, upcoming events, and opportunities for people to get involved. Through this website, Hussain aims to create greater awareness about the foundation's work and encourage others to contribute to the cause of helping those in need. It offers a user-friendly interface where visitors can easily learn about the various programs, donate to support the cause, and volunteer their time or skills. Hussain's dedication to making a positive impact is reflected in the thoughtful design and structure of the website, making it a vital tool for driving change and reaching a wider audience. Overall, the website stands as a testament to Hussain Akhter’s commitment to creating a more just and equitable society, where everyone has access to the resources and support they need to thrive.

Arha Help Foundation

The Arha Help Foundation aims to improve life in villages by addressing common issues like water supply and maintaining the village mosque. The foundation provides water coolers, assists with repairs and equipment for the mosque, and helps anyone in need within the community.

By bringing the village together, the foundation ensures support in emergencies or when someone requires financial or material aid, making the village a better place for all.
